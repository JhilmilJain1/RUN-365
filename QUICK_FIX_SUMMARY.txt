REST DAY LOGIC (CORRECTED)
================================================================================

Beginner:
  7 days selected → 6 training + 1 rest (OVERRIDE)
  6 days selected → 6 training + 1 rest (natural)
  5 days selected → 5 training + 2 rest (natural)

Intermediate:
  7 days selected → 6 training + 1 rest (OVERRIDE)
  6 days selected → 6 training + 1 rest (natural)

Advanced:
  7 days selected → 7 training + 0 rest (allowed)
  6 days selected → 6 training + 1 rest (natural)

Elite:
  7 days selected → 7 training + 0 rest (allowed)
  Any selection → As selected (no override)
